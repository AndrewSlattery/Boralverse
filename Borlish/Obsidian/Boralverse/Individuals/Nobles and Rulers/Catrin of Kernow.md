Catrin of Kernow (d. 1232) was an influential figure in Tetrarchic [[Borland]].
# Family
Catrin of Kernow was the elder sister of King Alan VI of Greater Devon, who orchestrated the [[Realignment of Devon]] in 1225. Their uncle was [[Pope Damian VI|Damian the Wise]], the first (Roman) Pope to be elected from among the Brethin religious order.
