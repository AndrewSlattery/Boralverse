Margaret of Iscombe (fl. 1435) was the wife of [[Mark of Tolose]] and the mother of King [[Ambrose III of Vascony|Ambrose III]] of [[Vascony]].

She married Mark of Tolose at some point shortly before 1435 N. Along with Ambrose, they had three other children: their first child [[Alexandra of Vascony|Alexandra]], and two younger children [[Victor of Astorga]] and Natalia.
