Vigo VII (fl. 1121) was king of [[Kingdom of Greater Devon|Greater Devon]] in the twelfth century, a major vassal of the [[Constant Empire]] under Emperor [[Roger II, Drengot Emperor|Roger II]] (and later briefly for Roger's son).

In 1121 N he invaded the south coast of [[Wales]], attempting a reconquest of the southeastern Welsh principalities, without the support of his liege lord.
