Ezio Carvallo (fl. 1860) was a Portingale author most remembered for his contributions to the development of the genre of [[Masquira romance|masquira romance]].

# Works
- [[Tejan de Masquira ou Jalico]] (In Either a Mask or a Suit), a 1860 work which would be the first in a twelve-strong series of tales following the vigilante thief Matthias o Grallon.
