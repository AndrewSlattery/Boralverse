Un Seir Deura Soffire (One Evening Will Have to be Enough) was a 1913 work in the masquira genre by [[Roun]] author [[Josephene Foilliers]]. Its riproaring execution of the classic masquira plot structure of schemes and opulence galore made it an instant success across the [[Drengot Collusion]] and beyond; this work was the first book to sell more thna a million copies in its first year.
