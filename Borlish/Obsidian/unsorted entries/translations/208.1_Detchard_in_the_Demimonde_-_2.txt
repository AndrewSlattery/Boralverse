208.1: Detchard in the Demimonde - 2

continued translation from the beginning of [real-life] book The Henchmen of Zenda by KJ Charles, a 2018 pastiche of the 1894 romp The Prisoner of Zenda.

Nos se collaborau accougl tras un ogtoçad de meis magn avoccant, fin a l'acceptau y rebateyaçon tars d'un ambasctour, e jo decideu de xiphar luïr sou un patron munfeyandessem.
We worked together for a highly entertaining eighteen months, until she accepted an ambassador’s carte blanche, and I decided to sell my blade to a more generous bidder.

Tre annað apres, cant jo jace n'un masmorrat Parijan ant gur dy descovart incoïbr d'un catoum solter e fatal de star sont e dy sojorn incöibr equal vars y thron mixallour, a my bel domn Fransc fo yon scaveu jo cos baurous, e luy la parsoloy casc zaloc eð ognt por m'errevir, stant un careç amaug.
Three years later, when I lay in a Paris gaol cell awaiting the inevitable discovery of a single, deadly proof of my guilt and the equally inevitable trip to Madame Guillotine, it was my pretty Frenchwoman to whom I wrote in desperation, and she who bailed and bribed me out, at eye-watering cost.

Untal interman y við varenastr, yon y hasart eð y lim son imman, vray amigtað son preçous e teun com diamant, e moraltað n'es for incommod.
Such is the demimonde life, where risks and passions run high, true friendships are as precious and as rare as diamonds, and morals are an inconvenience.

Acconnosc aun Sr Jasper Detchard e vos, ou sunt ou soluð, e luy le sta y segr cas par Michael Elphberg nell'oç harvest frige.
That is Mr. Jasper Detchard for you, take him or leave him, and that was the man sought out by Michael Elphberg that chilly autumn.

Michael, Duc a Strelsau, heir scon nað a Rudolf IV a Ruritania. Michael, volent y rey.
Michael, Duke of Strelsau, second son to Rudolf IV of Ruritania. Michael, who would be king.

Veram me storra l'exposson vos soupleyar n'oc hour.
I really am going to have to do the explanations now.
