28.3: jamir "complain"

jamir / íaňąm…™r/ [ Ě…źňąm…™ňź]
- (intransitive) to complain, grumble, lament, to express feelings of dissatisfaction or discontent;
- (transitive) to bemoan, lament, to express sorrow or discontent about something

Etymology: from Old Boral jamir "to bemoan, deplore", a northern dialect form descending from synonymous Insular Latin gemńęscŇć (a variant of Classical ingemńęscŇć). In intransitive use it is first seen in the Middle Boral period.
