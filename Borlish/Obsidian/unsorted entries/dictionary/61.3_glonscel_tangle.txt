glonscel /gl…Ēnňąx…õl/ [gl ä…≤ňą√ß…õw]
- tangle, snarl, knot, a twisted mass of yarn or other stringlike material;
- problem, dilemma, tangle, mess, bind, imbroglio, a complicated situation with no easy way forward

Etymology: originally in the first sense only, from Old Boral glomscel "yarn, tangle". This descends from Vulgar Latin glomiscellus "ball of thread", a popular diminutive of Classical glomus.
