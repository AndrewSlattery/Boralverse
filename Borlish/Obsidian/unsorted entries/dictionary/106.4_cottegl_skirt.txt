106.4: cottegl "skirt"

cottegl /k…Ēňątijl/ [k äňąt…™jl]
- skirt, kilt, an article of clothing that hangs from the waist and covers the lower part of the body;
- the part of a dress or robe that hangs below the waist;
- (metaphorically) jacket, cover, case, casing, outer layer, any thin layer that covers or wraps around something

Etymology: from Old Borlish cote»Ěl "tunic, kirtle", a pleonastic diminutive from Latin cotta "undercoat", from Germanic.
