96.3: mesteyar "disappoint"

mesteyar /ˌmɛ.stiˈjar/ [ˌmɛ.ˈstjɑː]
- disappoint, let down, to displease someone by underperforming or not delivering something promised;
- sadden, dishearten, to make sorrowful

Etymology: via Old Borlish from Vulgar Latin mesticō "I sadden", from Latin maestus "sad, gloomy".
