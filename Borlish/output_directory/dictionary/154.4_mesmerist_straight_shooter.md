154.4: mesmerist "straight shooter"

mesmerist /ˌmɛ.zmeˈrɪst/ [ˌmɛ.zmɪˈʀɪst]
- truth-teller, straight shooter, plain speaker, someone who is honest and forthright perhaps to the point of bluntness;
- clarifier, demystifier, someone who helps you understand things more clearly;
- realist, pragmatist, someone noted for seeing things as they really are without blinkers of expectation;
- whistleblower, leak, someone who reveals information that powerful parties wanted to keep secret

Etymology: 18th century borrowing from Scholastic Latin mesmerista "truth-teller" and mesmerizō "I reveal the truth"; this verb comes from synonymous μεσμερίζω • mesmerizō in Malchassian Greek, a derivative of μεσμέρι • mesméri "midday; (fig.) clarity, understanding".
