ensembr /ɛnˈsɛm.br̩/ [ɪnˈsɛm.bɐ]
- band, ensemble, orchestra, group, musicians who perform together;
- set, kit, equipment, apparatus, a collection of instruments of tools collectively employed for some purpose;
- outfit, kit, a set of articles of clothing worn together

Etymology: early modern borrowing in all senses from French ensemble "together; outfit, kit, music group". This descends directly from the Latin adverb īnsimul "together, simultaneously". The term is particularly used for informal or temporary groupings, as well as games and other entertainment.
