149.6: 28th Lexember - sontiçtað "linchpin"

sontiçtað /ˌsɔn.tɪtsˈtaθ/ [ˌsɔn.tɪˈstah]
- criticality, necessity, exigency, requirement, the state of needing something (especially when there are significant negative consequences for lacking it);
- linchpin, mainstay, backbone, keystone, sine qua non, a person or thing that is critical to a system working or even existing`

Etymology: borrowed from Scholastic Latin sonticitās "urgency, exigency" or a derivation within Middle Borlish from sontic "urgent, critical" < Latin sonticus "dangerous, serious". The word has been used to refer to a necessary thing or person since the eighteenth century.
