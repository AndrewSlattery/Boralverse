73.2: entrquïr "pause"

entrquïr /ˌɛn.tr̩.kwajˈɪr/ [ˌɛn.tɐkwɐˈjɪː]
- pause, take a breather, break, to stop work for a short while between jobs or similar duties;
- gather oneself, collect oneself, to pause in order to prepare oneself to continue;

Etymology: late Middle Boral borrowing from Latin interquiēscō "I rest briefly", adapted to native prefix entr- "between" and verb quïr "to put out, turn off".

Un hour alcheðr lor porreu nos entrquïr.
