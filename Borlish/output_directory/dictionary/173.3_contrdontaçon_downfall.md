173.3: contrdontaçon "downfall"

contrdontaçon /ˌkɔntr̩ˌdɔn.taˈtsɔn/ [ˌkɔn.tɐˈdɔn.tɐˈtsɔn]
- downfall, fall, doom, demise, defeat, a loss that is particularly noteworthy or spectacular;
- disgrace, humiliation, a sudden nosedive in public opinion of someone or something

Etymology: noun derivation from the verb contrdontar "foil, stymie, thwart". This is a pleonastic extension of now-obsolete dontar "thwart, defeat", which descends from Latin domitō "I tame, I vanquish".
