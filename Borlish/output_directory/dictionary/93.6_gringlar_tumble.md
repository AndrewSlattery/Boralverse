93.6: gringlar "tumble"

gringlar /grɪnˈglar/ [gʀɪŋˈglɑː]
- tumble, topple, plummet, fall down, to descend in an uncontrolled way;
- be in freefall, crash, (of figures) to decrease rapidly;

Etymology: Middle Borlish borrowing from French gringueler "to tumble, roll down", itself from Dutch cring "circle, ring".
