71.2: heu "appearance"

heu /haw/ [haw]
- appearance, look, image, the way something (especially a person) looks;
- demeanour, aspect, bearing, complexion, air, the nonverbal components of behaviour that are characteristic of a person

Etymology: from Old Boral hiu "form, appearance", borrowed from Old English hiew "shape, appearance, colour". Specialisation to the appearance and comportment of people occurred in the Middle Boral period.
