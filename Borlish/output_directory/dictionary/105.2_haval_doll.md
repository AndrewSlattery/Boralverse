105.2: haval "doll"

haval /haˈval/ [hɐˈval]
- doll, puppet, effigy, dummy, a (usually small or crude) three-dimensional representation of a person;
- mannequin, a life-size model of a human body for displaying or fitting clothes;
- model, mockup, miniature, a simplified or scale representation of something

adj.
- model, toy, miniature, simplified or scaled down;
- mock, imitation, fake, ersatz, not genuine

Etymology: from Middle Borlish haval "doll, puppet" originally in southern dialects around Pentrose, a borrowing from contemporary Cambrick haval "likeness, image; doll, effigy". In adjectival use potentially attested from as early as the sixteenth century.
