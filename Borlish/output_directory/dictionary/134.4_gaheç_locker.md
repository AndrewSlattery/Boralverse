134.4: gaheç "locker"

gaheç /gaˈhɛts/ [gɐˈhɛts]
- locker, a storage compartment with a lock for securing personal possesions in a public space;
- lockbox, safe, a secure compartment for storing high-value items

Etymology: Middle Borlish borrowing via Vascon gaheç "lockbox" from Arabic قَفِيز • (qafīz) "a measure of dry volume".
