72.4: confraðr "ilk"

confraðr /kɔnˈfra.ðr̩/ [kʊɱˈfʀa.ðɐ]
- colleague, fellow, peer, someone else in the same organisation;
- ilk, kind, sort, cohort, type, the category in which something exists

Etymology: Middle Boral borrowing from contemporary Latin confrāter "fellow member of a religious community".
