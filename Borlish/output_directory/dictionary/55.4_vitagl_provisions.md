55.4: vitagl "provisions"

vitagl /viˈtɛjl/ [vɪˈtɛ.jʊ]
- provisions, rations, supplies, victuals, goods (especially food) obtained for future use;
- livelihood, income, employment, sustenance, a means of providing oneself with the necessities of life:

Etymology: from Old Boral vitaȝl "provisions", a borrowing from synonymous Old French vitaile, itself descended from Latin vīctuālia "nutritional things". This is a derivative of vīctus "living, diet", from vīvō "I live".
