197.4: evolganç "leak"

evolganç /ˌe.vɔlˈgants/ [ˌe.vʊlˈgants]
- publication, spread, trending, the process of something becoming public knowledge;
- disclosure, divulgation, breach, the revelation of information previously held secret;
- leak, exposé, material which discloses such information

Etymology: noun derivation from verb evolgar "publicise, promulgate, leak", a borrowing from Scholastic Latin ēvulgō "I publish among the people", itself from vulgus "the common people".
