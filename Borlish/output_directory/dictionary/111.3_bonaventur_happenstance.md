111.3: bonaventur "happenstance"

bonaventur /ˌbo.na.vɛnˈtɪr/ [ˌbo.nɐ.vɪnˈtɪː]
- happenstance, accident, chance, coincidence, the chance or random quality of an event or circumstance

Etymology: a borrowing via a French language (in which the semantic shift occurred) from Lombard bunaventura "good fortune, luckiness".
