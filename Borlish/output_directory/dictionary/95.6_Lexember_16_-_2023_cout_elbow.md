95.6: Lexember 16 - 2023: cout "elbow"

cout /kut/ [kut]
- elbow, the joint between the upper arm and the forearm;
- (by extension) bend, any turn like that of an elbow as in a wall/road/pipe etc.,
- crank, a bent piece of an axle with which one can impart rotation;
- (customary, poetic) cubit, a unit of distance nowadays equal to two feet

Etymology: via Old Borlish coud, coft "elbow, bend" from Latin cubitum "elbow, cubit". This is related to verb cubō "I lie down, I recline".
