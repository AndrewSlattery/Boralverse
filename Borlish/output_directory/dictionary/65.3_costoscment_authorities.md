65.3: costoscment "authorities"

costoscment /ˌko.stɔxˈmɛnt/ [ˌko.stʊxˈmɛnt]
- authorities, law enforcement, powers that be, the bodies that have political, administrative or especially judicial power in a given sphere

Etymology: nominalisation from verb costojar "defend, police, regulate", an Old Boral derivation of noun costosc "protection, defence". This descends uncomplicatedly from Latin custōdia "safekeeping, custody"; compare Kentish costage "imprisonment, captivity".
