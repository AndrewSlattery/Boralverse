135.4: brevier "memo"

brevier /breˈvjɛr/ [bʀɪˈvjɛː]
- memo, memorandum, note, reminder, a short bit of writing to prevent one forgetting something;
- summary, overview, précis, synopsis, a summarised description of something;
- brief, report, a description of events

Etymology: Middle Borlish borrowing from Latin breviārium "short piece of writing", from brevis "short". Originally restricted to legal contexts, the word had entered common usage by the sixteenth century.
