56.3: reloðeç "finery"

reloðeç /ˌre.loˈðɛts/ [ˌʀe.lʊˈðɛs]
- splendour, grandeur, magnificence of appearance;
- refinement, elegance, gentility, sophistication, an esteemed manner associated with those of status;
- finery, regalia, bling, decoration or ornamentation especially of clothing

Etymology: noun derivative of reloð "fine, fancy, refined", which descends uncomplicatedly via Old Boral from Vulgar Latin relautus "elegant, splendid, fashionable". This is a variant of synonymous lautus, which is an adjectival derivative of lavō "I wash, clean".
