123.2: arveiðrous "random"

arveiðrous /ˌar.viðˈruz/ [ˌɐː.vɪ(ð)ˈʀuz]
- arbitrary, discretionary, unspecific, subjective, not based on any objective criterion;
- random, unpredictable, not conforming to any perceptible pattern;
- impulsive, capricious, spontaneous, acting without reason or forewarning

Etymology: seventeenth century, adjectival derivative from arveiðr "impulse, whim, caprice". This descends directly from Latin arbitrium "judgement, decision; discretion". In the sense of unpredictability the word is attested from the late eighteenth century.
