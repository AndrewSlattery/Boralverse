99.4: hayennaç "homework"

hayennaç /ˌhɛ.jɛˈnats/ [ˌhɛjˈnats]
- workload, allocation, assignment, duties, the amount of work assigned to a particular worker;
- homework, additional schoolwork to be done outside of school

Etymology: pejorative derivation from hayen "task, duty, piece of work", borrowed from synonymous haiena in Vascon. This reflects Latin facienda "that which is to be done".
