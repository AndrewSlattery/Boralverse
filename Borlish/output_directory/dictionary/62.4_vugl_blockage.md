62.4: vugl "blockage"

vugl /vajl/ [ˈva.jʊ]
- blockage, occlusion, jam, obstruction, something that is in the way of some passage;
- chokepoint, bottleneck, the part of a process that is slowest or most cumbersome

Etymology: originally in the first sense, a Middle Boral deverbal from vuglar "to choke, clog, jam". This verb is an alteration of earlier vugrar, borrowed in the Old Boral period from Old English wyrġan "to strangle, worry".
