126.2: scobr "bang"

scobr /ˈxɔbr̩/ [ˈxɔ.bɐ]
- bang, pop, the sound of an explosion;
- slap, crack, a sudden percussive sound

Etymology: from Old Borlish scoble, scople "explosion", from a Vulgar Latin form scoplus which shows metathesis from scloppo "slap"; compare Tuscan schioppo "gun", Barcelon esclop "woodblock, clog".
