- task, assign, set, to give as a piece of work to do;
- deal, deal out, hand out, give out, distribute, to share items among a number of people;
- (law) summon, subpoena, to require to appear in court;
- (slang) hook up with, tryst, to meet and have (often illicit) sex with

Etymology: Middle Borlish, borrowed from Latin assignō "I assign, distribute". The legal sense appears to be a clipping of legal Latin brevem assignō "I give out a writ". In the last sense the word is a backformation from earlier assignaçon "tryst", borrowed in the early modern period from French.
