97.4: luïcr "ridiculous"

luïcr /laˈjɪ.kr̩/ [lɐˈʝɪ.kɐ]
- ridiculous, risible, laughable, idiotic, unthinkable, amusing by way of being patently absurd;
- playful, sportive, unserious, recreational, not having more than entertainment as significance;
- trivial, trifling, pertaining only to minutiae;
- (of a match) friendly, played with no lasting consequences (say, for a tournament)

Etymology: Middle Borlish borrowing from Latin lūdicer "sportive, playful" with the stem remodelled on related verb luïr "to play".
