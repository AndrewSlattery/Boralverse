117.4: extollir "elevate"

extollir /ˌɛk.stɔˈlɪr/ [ˌɛk.stʊˈlɪː]
- elevate, raise, uplift, heighten, hoist, to cause to be at a higher position;
- promote, advance, to give a higher position in an organisation;
- upgrade, enhance, to provide with better features

Etymology: 16C, borrowing from Latin extollō "I elevate, erect, praise". Extension to promotion in a company occurred in the nineteenth century; to technology in the twentieth.
