92.3: ascreuçon "assignment"

ascreuçon /ˌa.xrawˈtsɔn/ [ˌa.xʀɐwˈtsɔn]
- assignment, allocation, the bestowal of a role upon someone or something;
- attribution, imputation, credit, the act of assigning someone or something as a cause;
- categorisation, classification, ranking, the process of arranging things into group determined by certain characteristics

Etymology: seventeenth-century noun form derived from ascreir "ascribe, impute, attribute, assign" by analogy with nouns like parceuçon "perception" from parcevir "to perceive". The verb reflects Latin ascrībō "I appoint, enrol, insert, add in writing".
