148.6: 21th Lexember - grivougl "scrawl"

grivougl /griˈvuil/ [gʀɪˈvuil]
- scrawl, scribble, chickenscratch, illegible handwriting;
- potboiler, shlock, hackwork, a written work of no artistic merit

Etymology: deverbal noun from grivoglar "scrawl, scribble, write illegibly", a borrowing via synonymous French grivouiller from Willem criblen "scratch". The second sense is attested from the early twentieth century.
