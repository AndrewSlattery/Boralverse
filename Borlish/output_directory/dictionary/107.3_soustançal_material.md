107.3: soustançal "material"

soustançal /ˌsu.stanˈtsal/ [ˌsu.stɐnˈtsal]
- material, physical, real, made of matter;
- physical, pertaining to properties of matter;

Etymology: borrowed from Scholastic Latin substantiālis "of or pertaining to substance", a derivative of substō "I exist; (literally) I stand under". Used to refer to a particular branch of science at the intersection of physics and chemistry since the nineteenth century.
