207.2: gouth "pushup"

gouth /guθ/ [guθ]
- pushup, press-up, a bodyweight exercise resting on hands and toes and pushing away from the floor;
- bounce back, comeback, rally, a return to form after some sort of setback;
- rebound, a romantic partner one dates in order to get over the end of a relationship

Etymology: late nineteenth century, during an early instance of the many physical exercise fads of the modern period. The term was borrowed from Welsh gwth "push, thrust, shove". The metaphorical uses (principally in gouth dar "to make a comeback, to attempt to get over someone by dating; lit. to give pushups") are seen from the 1970s.
