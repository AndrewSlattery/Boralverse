136.4: infruniscent "bored"

infruniscent /ɪnˌfri.niˈxɛnt/ [ɪɱˌfʀi.nɪˈçɛnt]
- bored, disinterested, unenthusiastic, not feeling entertained or enjoyment;
- impatient, irritable, restive, wanting to move on from what one is currently doing

Etymology: Middle Borlish, negated participle of the verb frunir "to have fun, enjoy oneself". This is a borrowing from Latin frūnīscor "I enjoy", which was used non-Classically as an intransitive verb. In the latter senses it is attested from the eighteenth century.
