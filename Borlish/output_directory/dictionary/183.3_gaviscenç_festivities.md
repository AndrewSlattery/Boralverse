183.3: gaviscenç "festivities"

gaviscenç /ˌga.viˈxɛnts/ [ˌga.vɪˈçɛnts]
- festivities, celebration, gala, party, a social gathering for entertainment and fun;
- revelry, merrymaking, mafficking, frolic, the act of having fun at such an event

Etymology: sixteenth century, a noun formation from the verb gavir "rejoice, have fun". This descends from Latin gaudeō "I make merry". Originally the word often referred specifically to the celebration of religious holidays or feast days.
