75.1: Astronomy Questions

excerpt in translation from scitation questions for the Safford Technic Scole's Spring 1989 course Along Natural Theory V: Astronomy from Lorenzo to th'Alluning.

Posc y closiscment dy Var Theudesc Primer d'an 1770, Saxony er mi ascenent e brouca auteç sur eiðel may all'ig seyon aut a veg calscon ne sy histoir. Cossireð y scaunç de Sneider sull'urbanfianç tost cohernt de Lepzi. Diçr alcot er sy scaunç influïð pall'oc e par y nuçal d'an 1772 entr luy eð Otto Sneider, scholer ag Sterncundhaus?
With the conclusion of the First German War in 1770, Saxony was ascendant and controlled more territory than it had at any point in its history. Consider Sneider's writing about the attendant early urbanisation of Lepzi. To what extent did this and her 1772 marriage to Sterncundhaus scholar Otto Sneider influence her writing?

"L'eutað Trevogt macquau un generation de domn e segr astr mirant e donc luy dabr es nis toð cas final vars y descovart vogder lonc mi plaç dy siecr novoç; y vecinanç dy planet Janus par bint Inám, eð y costregnç dy veloçtað normal, com bustr." Diçr alcot soð vos concordant all'oc inquit?
“The Voidtale fashion reared a generation of star-gazing men and women and thus was ultimately responsible for the cosmic discoveries of the mid-nineteenth century; bint Inám locating the planet Janus, and the normal speed being fixed, for example.” To what extent do you agree with this quote?
