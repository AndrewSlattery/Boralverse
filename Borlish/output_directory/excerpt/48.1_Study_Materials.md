48.1: Study Materials

excerpt in translation from the condensed study materials for the New Vithor course Concurrence History of the 16th Century: Death, Ships and the Movement of the Heavens.

Un bustr l'oc eutað dessur contradignt es Vascony; l'oc a graç a profit Novomondin des pelur eð autr enascment un aðoinç cas pouvlanç souvað de 1500 vars 1550 (Portingale noc ig inclus) com issent d'immigration.
One counterexample to the above trend is Vascony which, thanks to Novomundine profits in furs and other goods, actually saw in increase in population from 1500 to 1550 (excluding Portingale) due to immigration.

Y fin dell'Ascenenç Vascon con y Var dy Dou Quen vole denou mettr ag model feudal ne Vascony pre fos y siecr fis, portant.
The end of the Vascon Ascendancy with the War of the Two Queens would end feudalism in Vascony by the end of the century, however.

(Val svoccar ig l'oc no sta kynous y dicerrem vars y model feudal finir nell'aust, yon y ci concarnç islamesc de Kieu e de Cazany russesc souvartoy y nobiltað cos colombandessem.)
(It is worth mentioning that this was not the main factor in ending feudalism in the east, where the Islamic conquests of Kiew and Russian Cazany upended the nobility more directly.)

L'un effeit parcanç imprefaibr dy diminution dy model feudal sta y reitfianç de ley com cod suntuer.
One, perhaps unexpected, effect of the decline of the feudal system was the strengthening of laws such as sumptuary codes.
