67.1: Vascon Ascendancy - Exam Questions

excerpt in translation from the January 2003 N scitation documents for the course 1471 - 1517: War, Peace and Exploration after the Novomundine Landfall, 37th avenue in Concurrence History at the Tremonnow Open School

(i) L'eç entrou cort ac dessou souvr l'Ascenenç Vascon partenent con men a 100 parol cascun.
(i) Answer the following short questions on the Vascon Ascendancy in under 100 words each.

(α) Y plausiblessem es cal hom parmy ci plusour stant soumis com amour de Rey Ambrosc III?
(α) Who is the most probable out of the many people who are claimed to have been lovers of King Ambrose III?

(β) Cal cainç produgs y fonç notoir dy vascel Estellas daus Sanz, oc volent denir ja y scon nau solter acavant l'ocean Atlantic trasir?
(β) What caused the infamous sinking of the Estellas daus Sanz, which would have been the second lone ship to make the Atlantic crossing?

(γ) Carraçon n'au y Var por y Succession Barcelon un liesc a for 4 jorn?
(γ) Why did the War for the Barcelon Succession last for only 4 days?

(δ) Yon trocaurn Vascony den por ci oransc eð autr frugt citron accarr stovent par y Decret Sandrin?
(δ) Where did Vascony trade for the oranges and other citrus fruits required by the Sandrine Design?

(ε) Cant an er poesy vascon l'yamb a 5' exact primer benoçant la?
(ε) When was the strict iamb 5' first used in Vascon poetry?
