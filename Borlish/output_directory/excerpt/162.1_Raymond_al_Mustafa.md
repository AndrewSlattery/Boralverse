162.1: Raymond al Mustafa

collected notes in translation on Raymond al Mustafa.

Raymond al Moustafa sta un mathematorg e philosoph veunt a plaç meg ny siecr ogtoç.
Raymond al Mustafa was a mathematician and philosopher in the mid-eighteenth century. 

I reseyeu lonc un mesur ne Barcelon sou y patronnement dy Duc a Aragon.
He was resident for some time in Barcelon under the Duke of Aragon's patronage.

Durant l'oc epoc i corresponoy retrasiscent dy theorist soicer Rachel Desselut, e lorry pistol sovemblaurn certan entrell'idea volent tandem y sce de methodic signfeyar.
During this time he corresponded with fellow theorist Rachel Desselut, and their letters outlined some of the concepts that would go on to define the field of methodics [programming].

I passau sell'annað aval ne sojornaçon, souseyent cos final ne Brasil, no stant ancour un part dy polity d'Ambrosia.
He spent his latter years travelling, eventually settling in Brasil [Newfoundland], which was not yet part of the polity of Ambrosia.

Sy granfigl Fabian Mostàf denoy un astronom parnommað all'Observatoir a Paratzon, se sarvent de lodrignað mathematic ec defassað cos parçal par Raymond (com y despley ondatim por gyr celest augrimar).
His grandson Fabian Mostàf became an eminent astronomer at the Paratzon Observatory, using mathematical techniques developed in part by Raymond (like the waveform display to compute celestial orbits).
