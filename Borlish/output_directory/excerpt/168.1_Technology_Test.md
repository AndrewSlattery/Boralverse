168.1: Technology Test

excerpt in translation from the winter 2021 scitation questions for the Hulm Siebenhaus's avenue "Zwanzistewes Entwicklungen" (Advancements of the 20th Century).

(iii) Cal methodist laurant ag Schol Alçor de Nadacou acavau a y composson d'agr heredian preveir ne lorrell'ediçon d'an 1929, a dou dezein enter pre lorry prefacçon vole denir corroulað par experienç?
(iii) Which methodist at the Nadacow Higher School successfully predicted the structure of heredian acid [DNA] in their 1929 paper, a full two decades before their prediction would be verified experimentally?

(iv) Y stoir parachthon d'an 1968 Kenopleus, scaut pall'autour hamtant a Roun Ashford Pasquier, tien nis por y dob vanaglous a cal machinaçon modern orðenjar?
(iv) The 1968 parachthon tale Starsail, written by Roun-based author Ashford Pasquier, is responsible for coining the jargon term for which modern technology?

(v) Cal per de fraðr e sour instauraurn y colluy commarcer Pannier d'an 1903, oc volent tandem un presenç broucar ne 69 region politic nell'an 2000? Ðe feirn cossy ne cal citað lor Drengoçan?
(v) Which brother-sister pair founded the Pannier market guild in 1903, which would go on to have a presence in 69 political regions by the year 2000? In which then-Drengot city did they do so?

(vi) D'an calun caye y Torne Mondial a Lançal preu receut yonoscon ne Mendeva, e de cu vene y tourm gajan cavent all'oç cainç?
(vi) In which year was the Lineball Global Tourney first held in Mendeva [North America], and whose team was victorious at that event?
