182.1: History Quiz, 1900–50

excerpt in translation from 2021 book Excourse Puzzlers, published by the Royal Tommarth Castinghouse.

Ne cal polity Mendevan ern y preu boðour e diacustour sobottal orðenjað d'an 1903?
In which Mendevan [North American] polity were the first sobot [radio] transmitters and receivers invented in 1903?

Calun entrell'adolescenç de Dorotha Quistack, editað d'an 1917, gajannað l'Honour de Hatfell e se cof renastið com un lasc protagoneyað par Joke Pentawer pog dou annað aval?
Which of the adolescences [coming-of-age novels] of Dorotha Quistack, published in 1917, won the Hatfell Honour and was adapted into a film starring Joke Pentawer just two years later?

Cuscon dirigeu l'administraçon dont y sfondranç d'an 1925 sta y commet dell'Anarchy Portingal noncupað?
Who led the government whose collapse in 1925 was the beginning of the so-called Portingale Anarchy?

Comoscon denoy y lonctumn dy pie (degm) redefinið d'an 1946, le forniscent dy taus seyon aut destr?
How was the length of the (dime) foot redefined in 1946, giving it the value it has to this day?

Cu sta y monarch de Hasiny dont rumorað es ig y mort deut an mescainç conveyer d'an 1950 er volonter conjuraçonnað sou y saivenç de sy granfigl Peðer?
Who was the monarch of Hasiny whose death due to a convoy accident in 1950 is alleged to have been purposefully orchestrated with the knowledge of his grandson Pether?
