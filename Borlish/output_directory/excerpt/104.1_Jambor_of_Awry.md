104.1: Jambor of Awry

Jambor d'Avry sta un rey primal n'Avry pog asc apres dy succession dy polity des y principaltað ny siecr derran.
Jambor of Awry [~Hungary] was an early king of Awry following the polity's succession from the principality of the previous century.

I le gournau a mesur de spanjon d'influyenç Majoric, entrant n'union monarchic con y ci principaltað auster ne Haliç e Vallac, e denent un alley ag Roman malchassian ny souð.
He oversaw significant expansion of Majoric influence, entering into personal union with the eastern principalities of Halich and Vallack [~Galicia, Wallachia], and becoming an ally of the Malchassian Romans [~Byzantines] to the south.

Y gran Livrer de Sobjan er costroit durant sy regn, stant un gravatoir parmanent d'ouvr temporane.
The great Library of Sobjan was built during his reign, an enduring archive of contemporary works.

L'eç incluyen scrit mathematic des y Caliphað Sengr, eç auvorn ne proces de traducçon a latin.
These included mathematical texts from the Single Caliphate, being translated into Latin in great quantities.

Ci siecr apres, un dy descovan dy Livrer intermaneu cla por y Codex Mysarnos notoir descifrar.
Centuries later, one of the Library's manuscripts was instrumental in decoding the famed Mysarnos Codex.
