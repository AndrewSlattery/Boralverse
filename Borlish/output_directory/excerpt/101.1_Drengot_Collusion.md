101.1: Drengot Collusion

excerpt in translation on the subject of the Drengot Collusion.

Y Collujon Drengoçan sta un alleyanç-federaç de païs monfeyað n'Europ norðovester.
The Drengot Collusion was a multinational alliance-federacy in northwestern Europe.

Nommað lonc y ci Empir Drengoçan ançan (lou stant nommað lonc y linaç nobr normant ayent bempart de Gaul e d'Albion unfeyað),
Named for the older Drengot Empires (themselves named for the Norman noble family who first united much of Gaul and Albion),

l'oc crescoy des concort divers ny siecr novoç mi vars y possenç principal ny region denir fin a sy sfondranç dramatic d'an 1961.
it grew from various treaties in the mid-nineteenth century into the principal power in the region until its dramatic collapse in 1961.

Y Collujon Drengoçan smargoy parmy y polity francesc nell'epoc dell'Asteller Mondial.
The Drengot Collusion arose among the French polities of the Global Workshop period.

pog asc apres er y Nou Sar Urban (un congeries de citað autonom naðusc oc Londr sovran abrangent, tan com Ausbagn n'Istr Boral)
Shortly thereafter the New Urban Mesh (a collection of somewhat-autonomous cities including independent London, as well as Axbane on Borland)

caut sousunt—ben denuð autour, lonc y nosthubr idiosyncras seyon aut—dy Collujon smargent.
was subsumed by—or took control of, depending on one's personal biases—the nascent Collusion.
