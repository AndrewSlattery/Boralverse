125.1: Early Modern Morrack

excerpt in translation from notes on the polity of Morrack [Morocco].

Y Colluy dy Nou Mont nommað cos auspecer sta corporalleyað par Malic Mounir al-Hamdawi d'an 1643, e pres pront la nonçamen dell'Abanc Novomondin fos yent a cort ern vascel dy Colluy accaçað vars l'ovest.
The prophetically-named New World Company was established by Malik Munir al-Hamdawi in 1463, and almost as soon as news of the Novomundine Landfall had reached court were ships of the Company sent westward.

Cay l'oc flottisc ayent communeyanç fait con y gent dell'Arcabil, tan com y dou empir Mascic e Tavancin.
It was this fleet which made contact with the peoples of the Arcabil, as well as the Mashick and Tavancine empires.

Vascel Morrac stan y primer a y mont circonnavigar, stant parvenuð ag Gran Rajognt Borunesc d'an 1519 via Cappatia.
Morrack ships were the first to circumnavigate the world, having reached the Great Borunesk Meeting of 1519 via Cappatia.

Souspeut es ig y mort dell'Emperour Tavancin Carivasary d'an 1660 fo veraçter un assassinat accommis lonc commant Morrac, cos cuidað por y cæros ovrir d'un commarç cas argent profitandessem des Tavancy.
The death of Tavancine Emperor Cariwasary in 1660 is suspected to have been an assassination carried out on Morrack orders, purportedly to allow for more profitable trade in silver from Tavancy.
