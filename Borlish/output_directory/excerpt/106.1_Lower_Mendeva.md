106.1: Lower Mendeva

sentences in translation on the subject of Lower Mendeva [Central America].

Ny region es y polity a Mascic predominant, oc spanent entr y dou rimot e vars y norð aðief por poðir Mivoc toccar nell'ovest e Taisca toccar nell'aust.
The region is predominated by the polity of Mashick, which stretches from coast to coast and so far north as to border Mivock in the west and Taisha in the east.

Orðenjanç barric plusour dell'epoc d'Atelier jagerrem smargoy des Mendeva Bassem, deut plausibr a reclam addoçmental cas y respanment rapid d'accaç filer (no sceint pog adopçon paravant dy sar a gonfan e lucern por deslocar ny region).
Many barric innovations of the late Workshop period came out of Lower Mendeva, likely due to practical concerns around the rapid spread of wired post (there being little previous adoption of the flag-lamp steeplemesh in the region to displace).

Pre y siecr veintem jagerrem, l'oc region fo denuð enajour diçr de mansc com maus eð ambac.
By the late twentieth century, this region had become major exporters of foods such as mause and amback.

Març larc aun origin ne Mendeva Bassem, ec gles bilrod, scoclet, scadom e tascban abrangent.
Many trade goods originated in Lower Mendeva, including billrod dye, shockle, shadome and tashban.
