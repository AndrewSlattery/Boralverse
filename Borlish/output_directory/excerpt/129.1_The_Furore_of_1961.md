129.1: The Furore of 1961

notes in translation on the collapse of the Drengot Collusion.

All'hour dy Tourf, y Collujon sta gournað par un administraçon stanne sou Romney Darchild.
At the time of the Furore, the Collusion was being administered by a tinplate government under Romney Darchild.

Sceye parmy toð y scandal dy Tourf y descujon dy traitment furtif accommis par y Majon Roug noncupað (y nom commun por y Minister a Moustr dy Collujon) situað a Roun.
One of the scandals of the Furore was the revelation of the clandestine activities undertaken by the Red House (the popular name of the Collusion's Ministry for Information) in Roun.

L'ec gest, enombrað ny Brevier de Darchild, surcourn un kirt ag madou, cos primer l'administraçon actual dessoclant eð apres cos surrun y Collujon enter.
These activities, enumerated in the Darchild memorandum, lit the fuse, first bringing down the current administration and shortly thereafter the entire Collusion.

Un rebondisc dy sfondranç dy Collujon Drengoçan sta l'effectuanç dy reform Cantuesc cas ostracon, ec volen a lorry mesur sarvir com model ny polity plusour tras y mont.
One effect of the collapse of the Drengot Collusion was the implementation of the Kentish ostracon reforms, which would go on to be used as a model in several polities across the globe.
