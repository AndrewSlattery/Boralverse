Notes:
❖ Nouns will be presented in two forms: first their citation form, without any marking, and
second their unpossessed usage form, which contains the semianimate or inanimate marking.
Be sure to check the table in the grammar section for the exact forms.
➢ e.g. deną / ądeną (n. GX) - cobblestone
❖ Verbs and adjectives will be presented in only the infinitive/neutral form.
➢ e.g. pe (v. TR) - to speak, to talk, to say
❖ Adpositions will be presented only in citation form.
➢ e.g. ną (adp.) - in, inside
