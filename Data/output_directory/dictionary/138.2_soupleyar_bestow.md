138.2: soupleyar "bestow"

soupleyar /ňĆsu.pliňąjar/ [ňĆsu.pl…™ňą Ě…źňź]
- bestow, grant, impart, gift, confer, to present something to someone freely (especially as an honour or a sign of good favour);
- equip, render, supply, kit out with, to bring as provisions or equipment

Etymology: via Old Borlish sopleir "fill up, furnish, supply, provide" (later conflated with sople»Ěar "require, take, consider necessary") from synonymous suppleŇć in Latin. Used for gifts originally in the context of granting land and titles to people.
