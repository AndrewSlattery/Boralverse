59.2: travodar "negotiate"

travodar /ňĆtra.voňądar/ [ňĆt Äa.v äňądaňź]
- negotiate, treat, discuss, to confer with others in order to reach an agreement on some issue;
- barter, haggle, deal, to argue with a seller over the fair price of a good;

Etymology: backformation from Middle Boral travoda√į "negotiation, discussion, barter (n.)" as if a past participle. In fact the noun comes from Welsh travodaeth "discussion, commerce", from travoda "I handle, discuss, deal with".
