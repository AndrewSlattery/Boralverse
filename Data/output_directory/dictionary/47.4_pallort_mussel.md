47.4: pallort "mussel"

pallort /paňąl…Ērt/ [p…źňąl…Ēňźt]
- mussel, any of several groups of bivalve shellfish with long asymmetric shells;
- halfwit, dullard, someone perceived to be lacking in intelligence

Etymology: from Old Boral palorde "clam, mussel", from synonymous Vulgar Latin *palorda, itself an alteration of Classical pelŇćris, pelŇćridem "large clam or mussel". The word has been used as an insult since at latest the sixteenth century.
