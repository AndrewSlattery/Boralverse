229.1: Match of the Day

report in translation from the sports pages of a Tommarth daily, on the previous weekend's lineball matches.

Scein catr murðon stivað nell'arena ag ðaugr de Sou je Saðorn por spiar ig Badginton OG repous un offir voud d'Alster e retien donc lorry plaç sur y zantr dy canvaskel.
Forty thousand pack the Sow embankment arena on Saturday to watch Badginton OG hold off a furious Alster comeback and keep their place at the top of the league.

Y çod hospital se caun tramou doç a zefr durant y preu tarç, ðe pasn roug contr y sdruçol de ploy eð un parform compos dy joun Resketh de Badginton.y 
The visitors go twelve-nothing down inside the first third, struggling with rain slick and a confident showing from Badginton's young Resketh.

Posc y caffay, Alster se ponn pedesterrem sur vason caugtrað, e licol de Johns eð Anvelds parein l'enombr.
After the coffee, Alster find their feet on churned turf, and braces from Johns and Anvelds even the score.

Mer minut ig remanent, y laçour jaçol poll'OG Whitby recief y step de man e spingr y bal vars un vangart hermetic a deg droc.
With mere minutes to spare, the OG veteran catcher Whitby gets the leg-up and pins the ball for an unassailable ten-point lead.

Y heloit inelüibr dy cançon triomphal de Badginton gal y partejan n'eç tarn tras y toun fin an hour tart dy crevoscr.
The inevitable chanting of Badginton's triumph-song follows the supporters into pubs townwide late into the evening.
