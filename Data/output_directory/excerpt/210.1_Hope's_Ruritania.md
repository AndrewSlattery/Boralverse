210.1: Hope's Ruritania

continued translation from the beginning of [real-life] book The Henchmen of Zenda by KJ Charles, a 2018 pastiche of the 1894 romp The Prisoner of Zenda.

Ruritania es un picq reyaum se ðejant ag cour d'Europ, souðaust jagerrem de Germagn, un eiðel petty, montagnous, boscer e pittoresq ant un gra certain cas eðeglment graç a sy posson e sy tarmn defensibr ne pinnacr.
Ruritania is a small kingdom nestled in the heart of Europe, to the south-east of Germany, a mountainous, forested, picturesque little place of some strategic importance thanks to its location and defensible mountainous borders.

Tras y dou siecr y nuverrem l'es regnað sou y dynasty Elphberg, un famigl reconnuð por lorrell'ogl azur eð y cavel de roug fosc vraseyant.
For the last two centuries it has been ruled by the Elphberg dynasty, a family noted for their blue eyes and striking dark red hair.

Se poð flannar par y galleir de portrait ny palaç a Strelsau e se cendr con Elphberg ant ogl azur e cavel roug tras annað jusc lorry borjon com for duc.
You may wander through the picture galleries of the palace at Strelsau and see blue-eyed, red-haired Elphbergs going back to their humble beginnings as mere dukes.

Roug interman y colour de reyaltað ne Ruritania: lonc y veil cod, "Ða roug, lor rey!".
Red is the hue of royalty in Ruritania: as the old saying has it, “If he’s red, he’s right!”.
